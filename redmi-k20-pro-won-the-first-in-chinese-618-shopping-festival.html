Yesterday was the first day of JD.com 618 Shopping Festival. A number of popular new smartphones, including Redmi K20 Pro, went on sale yesterday. Today, JD announced the ranking of mobile phone sales on June 1. Redmi K20 Pro won the championship of mobile phone sales, as well as the championship of new mobile phone sales, ranking first in the price between 2000 and 3000.

Redmi in the middle and low end of the market share is quite large, then why do you have to do something about flagship smartphone? This is the question that Lu Weibing, vice president of Xiaomi Group and general manager of the Redmi brand, is often asked, especially after the independence of the REDMI brand this year.

On May 28, Redmi officially launched two new flagship products, Redmi K20 and Redmi K20 Pro, while updating the low-end product line to Redmi 7A. So far, Redmi has formed a complete product system of "high-quality and extreme performance" on the smartphone, which has realized the transformation from red rice to Redmi. Redmi forms the layout of the whole product line.

In fact, REDMI set a strategy early on: adhere to the ultimate value for money, adhere to high quality, speed up internationalization, prepare for the high-end market. The Redmi K20 series is REDMI's first performance flagship smartphone. After independence from Xiaomi, the Redmi brand took only 4 months to complete the full product layout from the high quality Volkswagen to the ultimate performance flagship.
