Interview with Founder and CEO of Huawei Ren Zhengfei In Shenzhen, China, Question 1

May 28, 2019 • huujuun

As the most of valuable Chinese Company we can find in China, We pay special attention to Huawei. At Huawei headquarters in Shenzhen，Ren Zhengfei spoke passionately for one hundred and fifty minutes with Chinese media. Valuefinding noted down 62 questions of the interview in English. we also annotated the answer given by Ren Zhengfei.

Q1 from CCTV: First of all, I am a reporter for live broadcast. You have a military background, you are punctual. I am also punctual. How time flies! Four months ago, we had an interview in this same place. At that time, the public all felt that Huawei had hit its lowest point. Everyone was so concerned about Huawei, and then there are many interviews from domestic and foreign journalists. We didn't expect today's situation to be more complicated, or even tougher, than it was four months ago. Just now, another reporter took out a photo of a bullet-ridden aircraft. I was also given this same photo. It is a photo of the badly damaged fighter aircraft. I heard that you like this photo very much.

Ren Zhengfei: I early saw this photo on the site wukong.com, feeling that the photo of aircraft was quite like the situation of our company. So I sent it to my colleagues. The Carrier BG thought the aircraft in this photo is similar to its situation and started using it for communications inside and outside the company. Later, we found that there were not many major problems with the Carrier BG after analysis, since we have been getting fully prepared over these past more than ten years. So our current situation is that we are repairing our "aircraft" during its flight so that it strive to fly back home.
