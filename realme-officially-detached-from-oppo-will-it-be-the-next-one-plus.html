OPPO is on the list of top four Chinese smartphone brands which is called "HMOV" . On May 1, OPPO officially announced the launch of its mobile phone sub-brand, Realme. In India ,Realme share the OPPO production line for manufacturing, and two weeks later launched the brand's first phone--Realme 1.

In less than 3 months, the OPPO, a sub-brand fighting redmi in the Indian market, has achieved remarkable results, with Realme 1 selling 400,000 units and taking a 1% share of India's smartphone market, according to research agency CounterPoint

And almost as much as most mobile phone sub-brands, Realme's fate eventually broke away from the parent company and operated independently.

Li Bingzhong, the former vice president of OPPO and head of OPPO overseas marketing, officially announced on Twitter that he would serve as Realme's CEO and operate the Realme brand, which focus on overseas markets, in the hope of providing young people around the world products with design trends and strong performance.

After the official announcement on Twitter, OPPO CEO Chen Mingyong also forwarded Twitter, and blessed:

Set out with enthusiasm, , bless the brand realme!

At this point, Realme took less than 3 months from birth, to launching the first product, to operating independently from OPPO.
